About us

We are the Bristol University Research Society (BURS).

We are a community within the University of Bristol that aims to provide students of all backgrounds with oppurtunities to discuss, showcase, and learn more about academic research. Our activities to promote and inspire students in considering research. consists of inviting distinguished speakers to give interactive talks, and arranging reading groups for different fields.

WHAT IS RESEARCH?

Research is what we do to extend the current knowledge we have on a particular field. This could be vary depending on different fields.

• Sciences - Coming up with new hypotheses and conducting experiments to see if they are true.

• Engineering - Using new techniques to build something which surpasses a previous benchmark.

• Arts and social sciences - Providing a new perspective or gaining more insight into something previously thought to be an understood phenomenon.
